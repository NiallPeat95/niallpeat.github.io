Niall Peat

I am a second year PhD student in economics at Emory University. I joined Emory from Australia having graduated with a bachelor of economics at the University of Adelaide. Before Emory I worked at public policy institutions in Australia including central banking at the RBA and tax policy at the Australian Treasury. My research interests are in applied financial economics and econometrics. I have experience in macroeconomic modelling and time-series analysis.
